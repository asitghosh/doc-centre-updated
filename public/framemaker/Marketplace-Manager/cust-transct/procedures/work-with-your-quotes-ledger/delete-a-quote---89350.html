Delete a quote

You can delete saved quotes that your sales agents generate.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Quotes> {quote} > [ gear icon ( )] > Delete a Quote

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    • Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Quotes.

      • Step 2. Click through Billing | Quotes.

      Step 3. Click a quote to see its details on the Manage Quote page.

      Step 4. Choose Delete Quote from the gear icon ( ).

The Delete Quote popup opens.

      Step 5. Click Delete.

      Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
