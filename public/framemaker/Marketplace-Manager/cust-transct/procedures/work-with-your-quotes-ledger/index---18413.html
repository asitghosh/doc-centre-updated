Work with your quotes ledger

You can view, search, sort, filter, edit, and delete saved quotes that your sales agents generate.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Quotes

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    • Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Quotes.

      • Step 2. ( Optional ) Enter a query in the Search field that matches all or part of a product name, customer name, sales agent name.

Then, either press Enter or click the magnifying glass icon. When your query matches one or more records, we trim the ledger to expose only the matches.

      Step 3. ( Optional ) Filter the displayed records by when your marketplace created them.

      1. a. Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to filter which quotes the table should show.

        2. b. Define the start and end of a quote generation date range by entering dates manually in the paired date-entry fields.

        OR
        Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon (         ) to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

NOTE: When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY ) or D/M/YY.

      Step 4. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
