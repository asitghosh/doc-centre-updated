Work with your payouts ledger

You can browse, filter, and search payout records. The table that describes them is one of multiple tables that all load together on a shared UI page.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Reconciliation (Payout)

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    • Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

      • Step 2. Click past Billing | Reconciliation , and then scroll to the Payouts table.

      Step 3. ( Optional ) Enter a query in the Search field that matches all or part of a vendor name.

Then, either press Enter or click the magnifying glass icon. When your query matches one or more records, we trim the ledger to expose only the matches.

      Step 4. ( Optional ) Sort the table by clicking any column heading.

Table rows are sorted by values in the column whose heading you clicked.

      Step 5. ( Optional ) Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to filter which invoices the table should show. Then, do any or all of the following..

      NOTE: Selections take effect immediately, and each effect is additive.

       
        • Do you want to filter records by their payout state?

      Apply a payout state filter

          1. a. Choose a state from the Payout Status list.

      The options are:

          • Pending
          • Successful
          • Failed
          • Manual
        • Do you want to filter records by their creation date range?

      Apply a creation date range filter

          1. a. Define the start and end of a payout date range by entering dates manually in the paired date-entry fields.

            OR
            Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon (
        ) to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

      NOTE: When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY ) or D/M/YY.

      Step 6. ( Optional ) Click Download CSV.

      Step 7. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
