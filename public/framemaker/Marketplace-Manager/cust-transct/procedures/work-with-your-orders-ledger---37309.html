Work with your orders ledger

You can browse, filter, and search order records.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Orders

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    • Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

      • Step 2. Click past Billing | Orders.

      Step 3. (Optional) Toggle your preferred view: Products ( ) or Editions ( ).

      Step 4. Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to for filter which orders the table should show.

The appearance and options here differ according to whether you clicked the Products view or the Editions view.

      (Optional) Search by order number

          1. a. Enter your query in the Search field, and then either press Enter or click the magnifying glass icon.

        • Do you want to filter the displayed records by their order state?
