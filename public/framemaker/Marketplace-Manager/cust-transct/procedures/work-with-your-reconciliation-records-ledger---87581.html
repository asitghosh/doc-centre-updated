Work with your reconciliation records ledger

You can browse and filter reconciliation records. The table that describes them is one of multiple tables that all load together on a shared UI page. The Reconciliation table does not show any search form.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Reconciliation (Reconciliation)

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    • Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

      • Step 2. Click past Billing | Reconciliation , and then scroll to the Reconciliation table.

      Step 3. ( Optional ) Filter the displayed records by when your marketplace created them.

  1. a. Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to filter which reconciliation records the table should show.

    2. b. Define the start and end of an reconciliation record generation date range by entering dates manually in the paired date-entry fields.

    OR
    Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon (

) to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

NOTE: When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY ) or D/M/YY.

      Step 4. ( Optional ) Click Download CSV.

      Step 5. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
