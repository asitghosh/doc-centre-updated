Work with your revenue shares ledger

You can browse, filter, and search revenue sharing agreement records across all products and vendors.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Reconciliation (Revenue Shares)

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    • Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

      • Step 2. Click past Billing | Reconciliation , and then scroll to the Revenue Shares table.

      Step 3. ( Optional ) Enter a query in the Search field that matches all or part of a product name.

Then, either press Enter or click the magnifying glass icon. When your query matches one or more records, we trim the ledger to expose only the matches.

      Step 4. ( Optional ) Sort the table by clicking any column heading.

Table rows are sorted by values in the column whose heading you clicked.

      Step 5. ( Optional ) Filter the displayed records by product sales activity dates.

      1. a. Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to filter which products’ revenue sharing agreement records the table should show.

        2. b. Define the start and end of an product sales date range by entering dates manually in the paired date-entry fields.

        OR
        Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon (         ) to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

NOTE: When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY ) or D/M/YY.

      Step 6. ( Optional ) Click Download CSV.

      Step 7. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
