Work with invoice details

You can explore the fine-grained details of any invoice, in isolation from all other invoices.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Invoices > { invoice }

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    • Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

      • Step 2. Click past Billing | Invoices.

      Step 3. Click an Invoices table row whose underlying record you want to review.

A modal popup opens, which cites the corresponding invoice’s:

      • billed user — including his or her full name, company association, and verified email address.
      • invoice ID — generally a seven-digit number.
      • payment status — PAID , UNPAID , or CARRIED. (Understand that PAID does not necessarily mean that a payment was submitted or processed. It can mean alternatively that no amount was due.)
      • invoice date — the calendar date when your marketplace generated the invoice.
      • amount due — the total amount that came due on the invoice in its most recently completed billing cycle, unless any additional amount was carried from an earlier billing cycle (in which case, the sum of these amounts).
      • customer ID — generally a four-digit number that identifies the billed user’s sponsoring (associated) company.
      • line items
      • associated payments — supposing that the invoice state is PAID and the invoice amount is more than zero dollars and zero cents (or whichever other currency your marketplace is configured to use)

In addition, the modal includes links and buttons that help you to:

      • preview or download a PDF version of the cited invoice
      • view details about each payment made toward the invoice, in the case that your payment system recognizes them as processed payments

 

      Step 4. Do any of the following.

        • Do you want to identify the billed party?

      Check who is obliged to pay the invoice

          1. a. Look for user and company citations under your marketplace or company logo.
        • Do you want to identify the billed product or service?

      Check which billable product or service the invoice covers

          1. a. Look for product and edition name values in the Description column.
        • Do you want to see what amount the invoice billed?

      Check the billed amount per billing cycle

          1. a. Look for the Amount Due value, in boldface type, a few lines under the PDF (
        ) icon.
        • Do you want to learn about any payments that the buyer has already remitted toward this invoice?

      Check the contract’s payment history

          1. a. Look for the Payments table at the bottom of the Invoice Details popup.

            Each row describes one payment.

      TIP: Click a value in the ID column to see details about the cited payment.
        • Do you want to download a printable (PDF) version of this invoice?

      Save a local and printable invoice copy

          1. a. Click the PDF (
        ) icon at the top of the Invoice Details popup.

      Step 5. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
