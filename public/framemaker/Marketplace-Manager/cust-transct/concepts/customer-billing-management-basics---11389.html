Understand billing information on a company details page

Navigation path:

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Customers > [ Browse by Company ] > { company }

When you click through any company record on the Companies page, Marketplace Manager loads details about just that company. From a financial standpoint, these details communicate:

    • What tax rate applies in the company’s physical locale
    • Exactly how much money the company has spent thus far through your marketplace
    • Real-time counts of all the company’s:
      • free trials
      • expired free trials
      • purchased products
      • suspended products
      • unpaid invoices

In addition, a series of inline tables on each company details page help you to search, filter, and track records of all individual orders, invoices, and payments that are associated with the described company.

Understand billing information on a user details page

Navigation path:

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Customers > [ Browse by Users ] > { user }

When you click through a user record on the Users page, Marketplace Manager loads details about just that user. These details communicate:

    • Exactly how much money the user has spent thus far through your marketplace
    • Real-time counts of all the user’s:
      • free trials
      • expired free trials
      • purchased products
      • suspended products
      • unpaid invoices

In addition, a set of inline tables on each user details page helps you to search, filter, and track records of all orders, invoices, and payments that are associated with the described user.

Furthermore, a set of user management options help you to make purchases on behalf of the described user.
