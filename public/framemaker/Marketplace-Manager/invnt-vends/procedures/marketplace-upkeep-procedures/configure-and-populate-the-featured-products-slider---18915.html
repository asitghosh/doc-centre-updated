Configure and populate the Featured Products slider

Navigation path

This procedure combines tasks that start at various navigation paths.

- Manage > Channel > Products > Catalog | Production Catalog > { product } > gear icon > Edit product
- Manage > Channel > Products > Groups | Product Groups > Attributes | Groups
- Manage > Channel > Products > Catalog | Sliders

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Mark a product as featured.
      1. a. Go to Manage > Channel > Products.

        2. b. Click past Catalog | Production Catalog.

        c. Navigate in the Production Catalog table to a product that should be featured.

        d. Click the gear icon in the corresponding row and choose Edit Product.

        e. Click past Profile & Branding | Listing Info on the Edit Application page.

        f. Scroll to the Categorization area.

        g. Make sure that the Featured checkbox is checked, under the Groups heading in the Attributes area.

        h. Click Save.

 

      Step 2. Turn on the Featured Products slider.

      1. a. Go to Manage > Channel > Products.

        2. b. Click past Groups | Product Groups.

        c. Scroll down the Product Groups page to its Attributes table.

        d. Click Groups.

        e. Make sure that the Featured Products slider is toggled On — to its Show predefined groups on marketplace setting.

        f. Click Save.

 

      Step 3. ( Optional ) Position the product in the slider.

      1. a. Go to Manage > Channel > Products.

        2. b. Click past Catalog | Sliders.

NOTE: The Featured Products slider does not impose any upper threshold on the number of products that it can include. However, we recommend that you populate it with no more than 12 products. Filling it with dozens or hundreds of products is not practical and defeats the purpose of any promotional strategy.

The Featured Apps table shows one row for each product that the Featured Products slider is configured to include. You chose these products in Step 1.

Toward its left edge, each row shows a list of numbers. Collectively, the active selections in these lists express the slider’s left-to-right sorting order.

Toward its right edge, each row shows a Remove button.

        c. Do either or both of the following.

      1. Click a Remove button to delete the corresponding product from the slider. You can do this repeatedly to delete multiple products from the slider. Trimming a product from a slider does not remove the described product from your marketplace or revoke any customer’s access to use the product.
      2. Choose a number to set the corresponding product’s left-to-right sorting slot. For example, choosing the number 1 causes the slider to position a product first in the left-to-right sorting order. Your selection is a fixed value that offsets the placement of other products in your slider.

      Step 4. Click Save.

      Step 5. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
