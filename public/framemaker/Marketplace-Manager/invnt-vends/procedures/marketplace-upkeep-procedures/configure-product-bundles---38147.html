Configure product bundles

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Products > Promotions | Bundles

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Products.

      2. Step 2. Click past Promotions | Bundles.

      Step 3. Click Add Bundle.

      Step 4. Use supplied fields to enter mandatory values.

  • Bundle Name
  • Company Name
  • Bundle Blurb
  • Description

Step 5. Use supplied forms to upload mandatory image files.

  • Bundle listing logo
  • Overview image

Step 6. Use the application-picker tool to specify which products the bundle includes.

Step 7. Click Save.

Step 8. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
