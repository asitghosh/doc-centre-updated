Approve or deny customer-generated content

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Reviews & Questions

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

      2. Step 2. Click through Home | Reviews & Questions.

OR
Go to https://{marketplace}/channel/marketplace?#approve , where {marketplace} is the DNS-resolvable server address.

      Step 3. ( Optional ) Click Approve N ( ) to approve all entries simultaneously, where N is the number of posts that await your decision.

      Step 4. ( Optional ) Approve or disapprove entries one at a time by clicking their respective ( Approve ) and ( Deny ) buttons.

      Step 5. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
