Edit the layout of product listing pages

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings > [ Edit homepage layout ]

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | General Settings.

      Step 3. Click Edit homepage layout.

The editing environment opens in your browser.

      Step 4. Mouse over any slider to highlight it and switch your pointer tool ( ) to a grabber tool ( ). You can:

      • Click-drag the highlighted slider to a new position on the page.
      • Click the trash can ( rubbish bin ) icon to delete the highlighted slider.
      • Click the gear icon to configure the highlighted slider, where configuration options sometimes vary per slider. Then, click Save Settings.

      Step 5. Mouse over any panel that is not a slider.

Your pointer tool ( ) switches to a grabber tool ( ) and you can click-drag the highlighted panel to a new position on the page.

      Step 6. Do any of the following.

  • Click Preview Changes to test your work without committing it.
  • Click Save and publish to commit your work when you are satisfied with it.
  • Click Back to Settings to cancel your work and restore the active layout.

Step 7. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
