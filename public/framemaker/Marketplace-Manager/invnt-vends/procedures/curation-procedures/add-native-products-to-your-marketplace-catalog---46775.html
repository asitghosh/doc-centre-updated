Add native products to your marketplace catalog

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Products > Catalog | Product Catalog > [ Add Products ]

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Products.

      2. Step 2. Click through Catalog | Product Catalog.

The Product Catalog page opens.

      Step 3. Click Add Products , and then choose Add Native Product.

A native product is one whose vendor has worked to integrate it with your marketplace directly.

      Step 4. Click Show Filters. Then:

      1. a. Choose No from the Added Products list.

        2. b. ( Optional ) Choose an option from the Integration Level list.

The options are:

        • Integrated — Your marketplace handles billing, service provisioning, and end-user management for the product, while also tracking all recurring revenue and dispensing negotiated payouts to product vendors.
        • Referral — Your marketplace does not handle product billing or provision any service. Instead, when a customer clicks Buy Now on the product’s marketing profile page, he or she is redirected to the vendor’s own website for purchase.

        c. ( Optional ) Choose an option from the Product Type list.

Depending on how your marketplace is configured and where it is deployed, the available options include at least some of the following product types, and possibly others.

        • Domain Provider
        • Download
        • Stacked Editions Web App
        • Web App
        • Web Application (manual setup)

      Step 5. ( Optional ) Click through an entry to examine its product marketing profile. Adding the product to your catalog exposes its profile page to all registered users.

      Step 6. Click Add in the row that names whichever product you mean to add — supposing that so few products are listed that you see the correctly populated row immediately.

      OR
      Supposing even your filtered results are so plentiful that you do NOT see the correctly populated row immediately, use the search form to limit the returned list more severely. Then, after you see the correctly populated row, click Add in the row that names whichever product you mean to add.

      Step 7. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
