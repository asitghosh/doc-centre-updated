Product marketing profiles can hide support content

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can prevent product marketing profile pages sitewide from rendering any content that would otherwise populate their Support tabs. This content could include URLs, email addresses, telephone numbers, and technical documentation PDFs.

The business case to hide such content could be, for example, that your organization is the sole vendor on your marketplace and delivers its support services elsewhere. Or the business logic could be that your organization sells only the third-party products for which it sells multi-tiered technical support service. Only your organization can determine when or if to hide support content that would otherwise be visible on product marketing profile pages.

Product marketing profiles can hide social media links

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that loads social media sharing buttons on product marketing profile pages. When the behavior is turned On, these buttons help customers to publicize any of your catalog products on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, or in email.

Product marketing profiles can hide product ratings

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that hides all user-submitted star ratings for a product until the number of submitted ratings meets or exceeds a minimum threshold. For example, you might consider it unreasonable to show fewer than 10 ratings per product. When the behavior is turned Off, all submitted star ratings are visible immediately.

NOTE: This feature is not relevant to you unless your marketplace is configured to show user-generated content.
