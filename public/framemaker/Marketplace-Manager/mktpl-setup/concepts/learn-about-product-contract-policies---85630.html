Understand product contract update policies

Active subscriptions can reject updates

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that prevents product subscribers from decreasing the number of contract-stipulated units on any product subscription that is in the ACTIVE state.

A subscription’s original contract can lock its minimum unit count forever

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that prevents product subscribers from decreasing the number of units on any follow-on subscription contract — regardless of its subscription state — lower than a number of units that the originating contract stipulated.

For example, consider a subscription contract that stipulates 50GB as the number of units. This originating contract lasts 6 months, and is then renewed automatically. Due to the automatic renewal, a follow-on contract is created, but the customer has updated the contract’s stipulations to specify 100GB as the units. In this case, when this marketplace behavior is turned On, the customer might reduce the number of units under 100GB but is prevented from lowering them under 50GB.

Understand product contract upgrade policies

Topics in this section cover the following subjects.

    • Active subscriptions can reject upgrades
    • Expired free trials can upgrade automatically to billable editions
Active subscriptions can reject upgrades

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that prevents product subscribers from upgrading any product subscription while it is in the ACTIVE state. An upgrade is the mid-contract transition to a more powerful or more expensive edition of the same product.

Related topics

    • Configure product contract upgrade policies
Expired free trials can upgrade automatically to billable editions

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that converts complimentary access to a product’s free-trial edition with billed access to its payable edition as soon as a consumer’s free-trial period ends.

NOTE: When enabled, this behavior applies exclusively to customers whose payment method is already on file.

Understand product contract downgrade policies

Topics in this section cover the following subjects.

    • Active contracts can reject downgrades
    • Active contracts can allow downgrades that extend their end-date
Active contracts can reject downgrades

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that prevents product subscribers from downgrading any product subscription while it is in the ACTIVE state. A downgrade is the mid-contract transition to a less powerful or less expensive edition of the same product.

Related topics

    • Configure product contract downgrade policies
Active contracts can allow downgrades that extend their end-date

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that allows product downgrades only when they extend a subscription contract’s end-date.
