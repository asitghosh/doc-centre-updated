Understand when activation collects the user’s phone number

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that forces the collection of a telephone number when registered users activate their user accounts. Your company might value telephone numbers as identifiers for a customer relationship management system, an external billing system, or some other purpose.
