Understand user content approvals

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that enforces channel administrator approvals for all customer-generated content, including all product reviews and any questions directed to product vendors. When this feature is turned On, a UI message — at Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Dashboard — notifies channel administrators whenever customers create any content.

Such a message could say, for example, You have customer posts pending your review. Review 400 pending posts.

Clicking the message opens the Reviews & Questions page, where channel administrators can approve or deny one pending post at a time, or, alternatively, approve all pending posts with a single click. Each approved post is then visible on the corresponding product profile page.

Understand product rating restrictions

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that limits which users can review which products. When the behavior is turned On, the only users who can review a product are the same users who have ordered it.

Understand product question visibility

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that hides all user-submitted questions on all product marketing profile pages. No questions are deleted.
