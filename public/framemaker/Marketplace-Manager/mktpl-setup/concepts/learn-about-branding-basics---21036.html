Understand where we use your marketplace name

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

A brand name that you apply to your marketplace storefront is visible in its UI text, page titles, and generated notification messages sent over email.

NOTE: Setting the brand name that your marketplace should expose in user-facing text is different than, and separate from, registering an Internet domain.

Understand the purpose of a favicon

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

A favicon is a very small image that acts as a sort of miniaturized website logo, which browsers can render:

    • in their location bar
    • in their title bar
    • next to a page title in their bookmarks and, sometimes, history
    • on the tab or pinned tab where a website loads in a tabbed browser

You can set the favicon for your marketplace.
