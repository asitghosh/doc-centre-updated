Why to enter a URL for consumer technical support

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

You can configure your marketplace to show two separate URLs where consumers can obtain technical support for your marketplace. The support center URL field is meant for use in linking to a community support forum or knowledge base and the contact support URL field is meant for use in linking to a support case ticketing system. However, you can use these fields to link to any support resources for your marketplace consumers, as long as you keep track of how your organization interprets their meaning.

Why to enter an email address for consumer technical support

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

You can enter the email address that listens for, and responds to, technical support requests from your marketplace consumers. Your team that responds when this address receives consumer support requests should include people with knowledge and authority to facilitate product orders, service provisioning, and billing.

Why to enter a phone number for consumer technical support

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

You can enter the telephone number where your marketplace consumers can speak with technical support engineers. Your team that answers this telephone should include people with knowledge and authority to facilitate product orders, service provisioning, and billing.

Why to enter a URL for the sitewide privacy policy

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

You must enter the public URL for your marketplace privacy policy. This URL drives a “Privacy Policy” link that some UI pages show in their footer.

Why to enter a URL for sitewide terms and conditions

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

You must enter the public URL for your marketplace terms and conditions policy. Registered users must agree to this policy when they activate their accounts on your marketplace. The URL drives a “Terms & Conditions” link that some UI pages show in their footer.

Why to enter a reseller URL for terms and conditions

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can enter the public URL for marketplace terms and conditions that apply only to resellers. Setting this URL is optional. However, when it is set, registered resellers must agree to the policy when they activate their accounts on your marketplace.

TIMESAVER: Entering this URL is of no use on a marketplace that lacks a Reseller Center.

Why to enter a reseller URL for sales lead terms and conditions

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can enter the public URL for marketplace terms and conditions that apply only to resellers who manage sales leads. Setting this URL is optional. However, when it is set, registered resellers must agree to the policy when they request to manage sales leads on your marketplace.

TIMESAVER: Entering this URL is of no use on a marketplace that lacks a Reseller Center.

Why to enter an email address for developer technical support

Navigation path

You can enter a developer relations email address for your marketplace.

Your team that responds when this address receives developer support requests should include people with knowledge and authority to facilitate product integrations, service provisioning, and billing.

TIMESAVER: Entering this URL is of no use on a marketplace that lacks a Developer Center.

Why to enter a URL for developer API integration documentation

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Vendors

You can enter the public URL that your Developer Center should show for API reference documentation that supports developers with product integrations.

TIMESAVER: Entering this URL is of no use on a marketplace that lacks a Developer Center.
