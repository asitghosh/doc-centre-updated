Configure whether user account activation collects a phone number

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Customer UI area.

      Step 4. Operate the Customers must enter a phone number to activate their account checkbox, next to the Activation label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Registered users must enter a telephone number during the account activation flow.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    The account activation flow does not collect any telephone number.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
