Work with Google Analytics

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

Before you begin

    • Establish, configure, and log in to a Google Analytics account. There, find and copy your Tracking ID (sometimes called a Property ID). This text string usually starts with a UA- prefix.
    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings. Then, scroll to the Analytics area.

      2. Step 2. Do one of the following.

  • Paste your Google Analytics tracking ID (as specified in the Before You Begin list for this procedure) into the Google Analytics Tracking ID field.

    Google Analytics can now process page tracking details for your marketplace.
  • Highlight and delete your Tracking ID value.

    Google Analytics can no longer process page tracking details for your marketplace.

Step 3. Click Save Settings.

NOTE: Data can take as long as 24 hours to populate.

      Step 4. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
