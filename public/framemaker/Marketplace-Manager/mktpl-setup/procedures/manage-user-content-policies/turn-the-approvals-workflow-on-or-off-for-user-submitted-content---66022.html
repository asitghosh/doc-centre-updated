Turn the approvals workflow On or Off for user-submitted content

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Reviews & Questions area.

      Step 4. Operate the Manually approve all user posts to the marketplace checkbox, next to the User Content label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    All product reviews, product questions, and product comments should be submitted to you, as the channel administrator , to reject or approve for posting.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    User-generated content should be published immediately without your consideration or intervention.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
