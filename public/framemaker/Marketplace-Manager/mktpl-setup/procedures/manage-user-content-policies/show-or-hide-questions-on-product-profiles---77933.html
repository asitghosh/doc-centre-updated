Show or hide questions on product profiles

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Reviews and Questions area.

      Step 4. Operate the Hide questions content for all products checkbox, next to the Questions label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Published product questions should be hidden globally.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Published product questions should be visible on the marketing profile pages for their respective products.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
