Set the return address for notification messages

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | General Settings.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Email Settings area.

      Step 4. Enter a correctly configured and active email address in the Send Customer Emails from field.

      Step 5. Click Save Settings.

      Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Set whether the sales agent is copied on notification messages for an assisted sale

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Customer Roles area.

      Step 4. Operate the Copy sales agent on customer emails checkbox, next to the Customer Emails label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox when sales agents should receive copies automatically of all billing-related notification messages sent to their associated accounts.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox when sales agents should not receive copies of such messages automatically.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
