Show or hide the employee directory UI in Application Manager

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Customer UI area.

      Step 4. Operate the Enable user profile and company profile page checkbox, next to the User Profiles label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    The users who are associated with a company can see and use the profile pages that describe them and their associated company.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Company and user profile pages are hidden from company administrators , billing administrators , and end-users.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
