Forbid upgrades to any active contract

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Contracts area.

      Step 4. Operate the Block edition upgrades for subscriptions with active contracts checkbox, next to the Contract Restrictions label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Users are now prevented from increasing the number of units on all of their ACTIVE subscription contracts sitewide.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    The only time when a user is prevented from increasing the number of units on an ACTIVE contract is when the contract’s own stipulations forbid such increase.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Turn automatic upgrades On or Off for expired free trials

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Payment Requirements area.

      Step 4. Operate the Expiration of a free trial will automatically upgrade to the paid subscription for all accounts with payment method on file checkbox, next to the Automatically Upgrade To Paid label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Free-trial product edition subscription contracts sitewide are upgraded automatically to a paid edition when their free-trial period ends — but only for the users whose payment method is already on file.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Free-trial edition subscription contracts should never roll over automatically to a paid edition upon expiration, unless their individual subscription contracts stipulate that they must.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
