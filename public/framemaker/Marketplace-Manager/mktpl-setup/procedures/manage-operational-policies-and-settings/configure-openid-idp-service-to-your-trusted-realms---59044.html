Configure OpenID IdP service to your trusted realms

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

      2. Step 2. Click Add an OpenID Trusted Realm in the Security area, next to the OpenID Trusted Realms element label.

An empty field is added to the page, immediately above where you clicked.

      Step 3. Enter a DNS-resolvable domain or subdomain whose IdP requests your marketplace should serve, such as example.com or sales.example.com.

      Step 4. ( Optional ) Repeat as many times as necessary.

      Step 5. Click Save Settings.

NOTE: The requesting system should direct requests from a white-listed URL to your marketplace at https://{host}/openid/id.

      Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
