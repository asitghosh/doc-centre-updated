Configure stealth mode

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Ordinary Internet users and search engine spiders should not be able to access your marketplace until its policies and settings are fully configured and tested. This is why you can prevent browsers from loading any part of your marketplace UI until their users enter special, sitewide login credentials that permit the server to initiate their HTTP sessions and process their requests.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Marketplace Password area, and then do one of the following.

        • Do you want to turn stealth mode On?

      Enter stealth mode

          1. a. Check the Password Protected Marketplace checkbox.

      The page exposes a Username text field and a Password text field under the checkbox.

            b. Enter a value in the Username field, such as MARKETPLACE.

            c. Enter a value in the Password field.

      TIP: We recommend that the sitewide password be a minimum of 12 characters long, combing uppercase and lowercase letters with numerals and special characters.
        • Do you want to turn stealth mode Off?

      Exit stealth mode

          1. a. Clear the Password Protected Marketplace checkbox.

      Step 4. Click Save Settings.

      Step 5. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

NOTE: A person who enters sitewide HTTP session credentials is not simultaneously logged in to any individual user account. The user account login prompt is at https://{host}/login.
