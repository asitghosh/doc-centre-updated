Reconfigure payment gateway settings, as needed

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Payment Gateway

CAUTION: Do not change payment gateway entries frivolously.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Payment Gateway.

      Step 3. Click Edit in the Your Payment Gateway area.

      Step 4. Enter new or changed values in the text fields, as appropriate.

The requested values differ by payment service provider and payment service product used.

      Step 5. Click Save Settings.

      Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
