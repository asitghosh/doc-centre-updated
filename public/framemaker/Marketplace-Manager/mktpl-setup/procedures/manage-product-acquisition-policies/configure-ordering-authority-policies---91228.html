Set an authority restriction policy for placing orders

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Purchase Restrictions area.

      Step 4. Operate the Allow only Company and Billing Admins to buy applications checkbox, next to the Purchase Access label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    No one can place orders on your marketplace except users who have the company administrator role or the company billing administrator role.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    All registered users can place orders, limited only by how much autonomy their company administrators allow them.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
