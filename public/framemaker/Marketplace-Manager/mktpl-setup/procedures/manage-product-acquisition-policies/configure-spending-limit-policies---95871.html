Set a daily spending limit per company

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Purchase Restrictions area.

      Step 4. Operate the Set spend limit checkbox, next to the Daily Spend Limit label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    The Daily field is exposed. Enter a maximum amount.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Any limit that was enforced previously is no longer enforced.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Set a 30-day spending limit per company

Navigation path

Procedure
