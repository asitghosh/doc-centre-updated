Set a billing details collection enforcement policy for free-trial products

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Payment Requirements area.

      Step 4. Click one option button, next to the Require Billing Information for Free Trials label, as follows.

  • Enable — The Billing Information page is present in the purchase flow for free-trial editions. No one can order a free-trial edition without entering his or her billing information.
  • Disable — The Billing Information page is removed from the purchase flow for free-trial editions. Any registered user can order a free-trial edition without entering his or her billing information.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Set a billing details collection enforcement policy for all billable and non-billable products

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Payment Requirements area.

      Step 4. Click one option button, next to the Require Billing Information for all Apps label, as follows.

  • Enable — The Billing Information page is always present in the purchase flow. No one can order any product without entering his or her billing information.
  • Disable — The Billing Information page is removed from the purchase flow for all free products, free-trial editions, and all fully discounted orders. Any registered user can order a free edition, a free-trial edition, or a fully discounted edition without entering his or her billing information.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
