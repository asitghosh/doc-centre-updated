Turn one-click access On or Off for no-cost products

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

TIP: A one-click product does not collect any billing details during checkout.

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Subscriptions area.

      Step 4. Operate the Customers can start free trials or buy free products in one click from the product profile page checkbox, next to the One Click Free Trials label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    One-click ordering is turned On for no-cost products.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    One-click ordering is turned Off for all products.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
