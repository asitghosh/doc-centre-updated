Set whether partially consumed fixed-price discounts can persist as credit

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Discounts area.

      Step 4. Operate the Allow fixed-price discount as a credit checkbox, next to the Credit label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Any remaining balance on a discount after its partial redemption shall persist as credit.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Any remaining balance on a discount after its partial redemption is lost.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
