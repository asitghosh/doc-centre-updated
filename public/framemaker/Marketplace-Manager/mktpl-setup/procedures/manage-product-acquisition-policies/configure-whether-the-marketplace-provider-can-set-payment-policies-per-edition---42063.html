Configure whether the marketplace provider can set payment policies per edition

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Payment Requirements area.

      Step 4. Operate the Edition level overwrite on these settings checkbox, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Channel administrators , including you, can set payment policies on an edition-by-edition basis.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Channel administrators should not have the authority to set payment policies per edition.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
