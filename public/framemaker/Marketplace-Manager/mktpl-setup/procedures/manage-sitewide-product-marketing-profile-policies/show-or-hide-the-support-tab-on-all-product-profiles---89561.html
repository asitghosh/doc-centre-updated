Show or hide the Support tab on all product profiles

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Customer UI area.

      Step 4. Operate the Hide support content for all products checkbox, next to the Product Support label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    The Support tab is suppressed sitewide on all product profile pages.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Product profile pages includes a Support tab when their vendor entered any support details during product integration.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
