Set the marketplace logo

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | General Settings.

      Step 3. Scroll to the About area.

      Step 4. Do either of the following.

  • Drag and drop an image file to the Marketplace Logo zone from any of your client system’s mounted volumes.

    OR
  • Click Browse , under the Marketplace Logo drag zone. Then, when the File Upload window opens:
    • Navigate through your client system’s mounted volumes and into the folder (subdirectory) that contains the logo image.
    • Click the image file once to select it.
    • Click Open.

TIP: The aspect ratio of your logo image should be 2:1. Its width should be exactly 200px and its height exactly 100px. We support PNG and GIF images but recommend PNG.

      Step 5. Click Save Settings.

      Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
