Set the marketplace provider name

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | General Settings.

      Step 3. Scroll to the About area.

      Step 4. Enter your employer’s legal operating name in the Company Name field.

TIP: For example, when the provider of your marketplace is a corporation, enter the corporation’s name. Or, when the provider is a governmental agency, enter the agency’s name.

      Step 5. Click Save Settings.

      Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
