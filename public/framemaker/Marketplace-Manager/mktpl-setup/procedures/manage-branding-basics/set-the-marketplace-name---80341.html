Set the marketplace name

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | General Settings.

      Step 3. Scroll to the About area.

      Step 4. Enter your marketplace brand name in the Marketplace Name field.

TIP: This differs from your employerâ€™s legal operating name. For example, suppose that the provider of your marketplace is called Massive Dynamic. Even so, you might enter Get XaaSy or Deals Cloudstore in the Marketplace Name field.

      Step 5. Click Save Settings.

      Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
