Turn sales support authority On or Off to disable companies

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Roles

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Roles.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Sales Support Permissions area.

      Step 4. Operate the Disable company toggle, as follows.

  • Slide the toggle right (
) to turn On the described behavior.

OR
      • Slide the toggle left ( ) to turn Off the described behavior.

      Step 5. Click Save Settings.

      Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
