Set the approvals policy for assisted sales

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Customer Roles area.

      Step 4. Click one option button, next to the Sales Agent Approval label, as follows.

  • Sales Agents never need customer approval — Sales agents can start and complete assisted sales without user approval.
  • Sales Agents always need customer approval — Sales agents must obtain user consent before completing an assisted sale.
  • Sales Agents can choose whether customer approval is needed — You grant sales agents the discretion to choose on a case-by-case basis whether to obtain user consent for an assisted sale.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Turn sales agent authority On or Off to enter payment details

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Customer Roles area.

      Step 4. Operate the Allows Sales Agents to enter payment details on behalf of a customer checkbox, next to the Sales Agent Payment label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Sales agents are empowered to enter payment details on behalf of consumers during assisted sales.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Sales agents are not empowered to enter payment details on behalf of consumers during assisted sales.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
