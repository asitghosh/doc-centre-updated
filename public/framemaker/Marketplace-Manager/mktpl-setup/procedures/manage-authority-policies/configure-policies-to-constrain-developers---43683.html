Turn developer authority On or Off to access customer data

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Vendors

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Vendors.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Vendor Portal area.

      Step 4. Operate the Hide customer data in vendor portal checkbox, next to the Customer Access label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    The Customers tab is hidden in Developer Center, and all billing tables in Developer Center omit customer-identifying details.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Developer Center shows the Customers tab and billing tables in Developer Center include details that identify customers.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Turn developer authority On or Off to manage sales leads

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Vendors

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Vendors.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Vendor Portal area.

      Step 4. Operate the Vendors can manage leads generated on their product profiles checkbox, next to the Lead Management label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Lead generation from product profile pages is enabled, empowering developers to see and manage any leads that their own products generate.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Developers are prevented from seeing or managing any leads whatsoever.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Turn developer authority On or Off to toggle product visibility

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Vendors

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Vendors.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Vendor Portal area.

      Step 4. Operate the Allow vendors to toggle visibility of their published products checkbox, next to the Visibility label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Developers see and can use a visibility toggle in Developer Center for each of their published products.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Developer Center does not show a visibility toggle to developers for any of their published products.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Turn developer authority On or Off to enforce advanced contract stipulations

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Contracts area.

      Step 4. Operate the Enable advanced options for subscription changes with contracts checkbox, next to the Contract Restrictions label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Many additional checkboxes are then visible.
    • Allow vendors to configure contract restrictions per price — When checked, empowers developers to prevent any change to the number of units that a consumer orders upon establishing a contract.
    • Block edition upgrades for subscriptions with active contracts — When checked, empowers developers to prevent any upgrade to the subscription associated with an active contract.
    • Block edition downgrades for subscriptions with active contracts — When checked, empowers developers to prevent any downgrade to the subscription associated with an active contract.
    • Allow increase in contract duration with edition to take priority over price in downgrade scenario — When checked, empowers developers to recalculate the relative rank of two editions from the same product, dynamically, so that an increased contract duration amounts to an increased rank for purposes of determining whether a contract change is a downgrade.
    • Block decreases on units purchased for subscriptions with active contracts — When checked, empowers developers to prevent any unit reductions for the subscription associated with an active contract.
    • Block decreases on units purchased on original contract — When checked, empowers developers to prevent any unit reductions below the quantity or volume that a consumer’s originating subscription order stipulated for an active contract.

      OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    All of the nested checkboxes that could otherwise be used to enable advanced options are now hidden, and developers lack authority to impose any of the advanced stipulations that the checkboxes could otherwise activate.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Turn developer authority On or Off to enforce edition restrictions

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Subscriptions area.

      Step 4. Operate the Enable edition level subscription restrictions checkbox, next to the Edition Restrictions label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Developers can see and use options in Developer Center to set advanced contract stipulations for any of their product editions.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Developers cannot impose any advanced contract stipulations per product edition.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Turn developer authority On or Off to rank product editions

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Subscriptions area.

      Step 4. Operate the Use edition rank checkbox, next to the Subscription changes label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Developers can see and use UI elements to set a rank, respectively, for each of their product editions. Edition ranks override order pricing in the determination of whether a changed contract constitutes an upgrade or downgrade.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Developers cannot set edition ranks manually.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
