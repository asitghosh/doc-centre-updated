Turn customer support authority On or Off to disable companies

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Roles

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Roles.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Customer Support Permissions area.

      Step 4. Operate the Disable company toggle, as follows.

  • Slide the toggle right (
) to turn On the described behavior.

Turn customer support authority On or Off to ‘view as’ for technical support

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

      2. Step 2. Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Security area.

      Step 4. Operate the Allow channel roles to request access to customer accounts checkbox, next to the Support Access label, as follows.

  • Check the checkbox.
    Customer support representatives are empowered to ask any registered user for access to the user’s account. Supposing that a user allows this access, the support representative can take any action on the user’s behalf.

    OR
  • Clear the checkbox.
    Customer support representatives lack any way to request access to a user account.

Step 5. Click Save Settings.

Step 6. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
