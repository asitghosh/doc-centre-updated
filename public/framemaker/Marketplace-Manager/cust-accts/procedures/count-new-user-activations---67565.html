Count new user activations

You can use a dashboard gauge to see quickly how many registered user accounts were activated recently on your marketplace.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Dashboard | Signups

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

      2. Step 2. Click past Home | Dashboard.

      Step 3. Scroll to the Signups gauge.

A line graph illustrates the recent ebb and flow of user account activations.

      Step 4. ( Optional ) Click Download CSV to export the set of records that underlie the line graph.

      Step 5. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
