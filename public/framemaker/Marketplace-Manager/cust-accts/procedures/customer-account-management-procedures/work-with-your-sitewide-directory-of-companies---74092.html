Work with your sitewide directory of companies

As a channel administrator , you can search, filter, and view a list of all registered companies on your marketplace.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Companies

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

      2. Step 2. Click past Home | Companies.

      Step 3. Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to limit which companies the table should show.

NOTE: Filter selections take effect immediately. Each effect is additive that does not negate a previous selection.

      Step 4. ( Optional ) Use any combination of company-typing filters until you find the companies that matter to you.

      Active

      Filter to include active companies

          1. a. Choose Yes from the Activated list.

      Filter to exclude active companies

          1. a. Choose No from the Activated list.

      Channel

      Filter to include companies with channel access

          1. a. Choose Only Admins from the Channel Status list.

      Filter to exclude companies with channel access

          1. a. Choose Only Non-Admins from the Channel Status list.

      Enabled

      Filter to include enabled companies

          1. a. Choose Yes from the Enabled list.

      Filter to exclude enabled companies

          1. a. Choose No from the Enabled list.

      Reseller

      Filter to include companies with reseller access

          1. a. Choose Only Resellers from the Vendor Status list.

      Vendor

      Filter to include companies with vendor access

          1. a. Choose Only Vendors from the Vendor Status list.

      Filter to exclude companies with vendor access

          1. a. Choose Only Non Vendors from the Vendor Status list.

      Step 5. ( Optional ) Use other features to find companies that matter to you.

      Date range

      List companies that match a creation date range

          1. a. Define the start and end of a company creation date range by entering dates manually in the paired date-entry fields.

            OR
            Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

      NOTE: When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY or D/M/YY.

      Search

      Search by text string

          1. a. Enter your query in the Search field, and then either press Enter or click the magnifying glass icon.

      When your query matches one or more records, we trim the table to expose only these matches.

      Step 6. ( Optional ) Click Download CSV to save a local copy of the table’s underlying records as you have trimmed them.

      Step 7. ( Optional ) Click a table row to show more in-depth details from its underlying record.

      Step 8. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
