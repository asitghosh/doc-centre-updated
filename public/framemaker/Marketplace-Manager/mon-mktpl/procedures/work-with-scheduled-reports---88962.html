Work with scheduled reports

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Reports

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

      2. Step 2. Click through Home | Reports.

      Step 3. ( Optional ) Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to filter which reports the table should show. Then, do any or all of the following.

      NOTE: Filter selections take effect immediately, and each effect is additive.

       
        • Do you want to filter the reports list by report type?

      Apply a report type filter

          1. a. Choose an option from the Report Type list.
        • Do you want to filter the reports list by the reporting period start date?

      Apply a reporting period start date filter

          1. a. Limit the oldest record that a report should include by setting its date range manually in the paired date-entry fields labeled ‘Start Date between.’

            OR
            Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon (
        ) to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

      NOTE: When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY ) or D/M/YY.
        • Do you want to filter the reports list by the reporting period end date?

      Apply a reporting period end date filter

          1. a. Limit the newest record that a report should include by setting its date range manually in the paired date-entry fields labeled ‘End Date between.’

            OR
            Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon (
        ) to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

      NOTE: When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY ) or D/M/YY.

      Step 4. Click a report to download it for offline processing.

      Step 5. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
