Essential templates are always turned On

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Custom UI | Customer Emails

Some templates are optional, meaning that you can deactivate them easily.

Essential templates, on the other hand, are always turned On and do not feature any means to be turned Off. Such templates are fundamental to the daily operation of your marketplace. We encourage you to populate their content first, before you consider populating any of your other templates. However, you can safely disregard any product-specific templates for products that your marketplace does not sell.

In contrast, each optional template shows a checkbox ( ) that you can check or clear at any time to turn On or Off. A template that is turned Off does not send any notification messages, even after a triggering event occurs.

Why does this matter?

Minus the information that generated notification messages are designed to communicate, registered users cannot always use your marketplace and its product offerings effectively.

We recommend that you leave all templates turned On, except the ones for products that your marketplace does not sell. Otherwise, when you turn Off templates that relate to your product offerings and their users, your avoidable technical support expenses can climb quickly.
