Work with the Sales Opportunity Approved template

You can edit content in the Sales Opportunity Approved template and turn its output On or Off. Because this template is optional, you can stop it from sending email notification messages.

    • When the template is turned On, it sends an informational message to any reseller whose sales opportunity submission was approved by the reseller manager.
    • When the template is turned Off, the reseller does not receive this formal notification.
    • Similarly, the message recipient can stop receiving the generated message by clicking its automatically injected “Unsubscribe” link. You, the channel administrator , configure the link’s phrasing globally for all message templates that incorporate it.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Settings > Custom UI | Customer Notifications > Accounts | Sales Opportunity Approved

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.
    • Make sure that your marketplace has the reseller user role enabled.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Settings > Custom UI | Customer Notifications.

      2. Step 2. Click Sales Opportunity Approved in the Accounts area.

      Step 3. ( Optional ) Slide the Enabled toggle right to turn the template On.

      OR
      Slide the Enabled toggle left to turn the template Off.

      Step 4. Enter an appropriate email subject line in the Subject field. For example, your subject line might be: Sales opportunity approved.

      Step 5. Compose an appropriate message, which uses any or all of the template’s supported parameters. Each parameter injects variable and dynamic text into a generated message, based on the combination of all relevant factors.

NOTE: Each generated message is customized for relevance to the context that triggered it. When any parameter calls upon a database field that is not populated in the necessary context, the generated message shows the parameter label instead of a null value.

      • {BASE_URL} — Inserts an HTTPS link to the fully qualified, DNS-resolvable server hostname that loads your marketplace.
      • {MANAGE_CUSTOMER_COMPANY_LINK} — Inserts the navigable web address where the reseller can start managing the company whose sales opportunity he or she qualified.
      • {OPPORTUNITY_COMMENT} — Inserts the full and verbatim message that the reseller manager entered while approving the reseller’s sales opportunity submission.
      • {OPPORTUNITY_COMPANY_NAME} — Inserts the name of the company whose sales opportunity the reseller qualified.
      • {PARTNER_LABEL} — Inserts the name of the organization whose channel administrators operate and manage your marketplace.
      • {PARTNER_STORE_LABEL} — Inserts your marketplace brand name, such as Acme Marketplace.
      • {RESELLER_COMPANY_NAME} — Inserts the name of whichever company association was the reseller’s , or the sales support representative’s, login context while he or she completed the described work.
      • {RESELLER_EMAIL} — Inserts the same email address that is the reseller’s , or the sales support representative’s, username on your marketplace.
      • {RESELLER_FIRST_NAME} — Inserts the reseller’s , or the sales support representative’s, given name.
      • {RESELLER_FULL_NAME} — Inserts the reseller’s , or the sales support representative’s, full name.
      • {RESELLER_LAST_NAME} — Inserts the reseller’s , or the sales support representative’s, surname.
      • {SUPPORT_EMAIL} — Inserts the email address that receives technical support requests at your organization. You populate this parameter when you enter a Support Email value in the Support Resources area at Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings.
      • {SUPPORT_PHONE} — Inserts the telephone number that receives technical support calls at your organization. You populate this parameter when you enter a Support Phone value in the Support Resources area at Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings.
      • {SUPPORT_URL} — Inserts the navigable web address where your organization hosts a knowledge base, peer-to-peer support forum, paid customer support agents, or similar resources for your marketplace. You populate this parameter when you enter a Contact Support URL value in the Support Resources area at Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings.
      • {UPDATED_BY_COMPANY_NAME} — Inserts the name of whichever registered company updated the described record most recently.

 

Then, use the provided toolbar to format your message body. As needed, you can edit its underlying source by clicking ( Edit HTML Source ). You can even insert graphics — such as a corporate logo — by clicking ( Insert/Edit Image ).

 

      Step 6. ( Optional ) Click Send a Sample Email to send yourself a copy of the generated message with all of its parameters populated.

TIP: The Subject field and message body field must each contain at least one character, or the Send a Sample Email button does not have any effect when clicked.

      Step 7. Click Save to save your work.

      Step 8. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
