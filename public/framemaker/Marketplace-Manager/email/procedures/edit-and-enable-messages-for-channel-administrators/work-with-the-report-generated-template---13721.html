Work with the Report Generated template

You cannot edit the message body in the Report Generated template or apply any formatting to it. However, you can turn the template’s output On or Off. Because this template is optional, you can stop it from sending email notification messages.

    • When the template is turned On, it sends an informational message to you, the channel administrator , whenever AppDirect generates a scheduled, recurring report for your marketplace.
    • When the template is turned Off, you are not notified.
    • Similarly, you can stop receiving the generated message by clicking its automatically injected “Unsubscribe” link. You configure the link’s phrasing globally for all message templates that incorporate it.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Settings > Custom UI | Customer Notifications > Marketplace | Report Generated

Before you begin

    • Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Settings > Custom UI | Customer Notifications.

      2. Step 2. Click Report Generated in the Marketplace area.

      Step 3. ( Optional ) Slide the Enabled toggle right to turn the template On.

      OR
      Slide the Enabled toggle left to turn the template Off.

      Step 4. Enter an appropriate email subject line in the Subject field. For example, your subject line might be: A new report is now ready for {PARTNER_STORE_LABEL}.

NOTE: You cannot customize or reformat the message body for this template. Its content is predetermined, and varies by the report type.

      Step 5. ( Optional ) Click Send a Sample Email to send yourself a copy of the generated message with all of its parameters populated.

TIP: The Subject field and message body field must each contain at least one character, or the Send a Sample Email button does not have any effect when clicked.

      Step 6. Click Save to save your work.

      Step 7. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
