Find products

AppDirect supports many approaches to product discovery. You can use whichever approach works best for you in the moment or combine approaches to become an expert.

Browse product listings

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to https:// {marketplace} /listing , where {marketplace} is the fully qualified and DNS resolvable server name for a marketplace that you use.

      2. Step 2. Depending on the visual design of your marketplace, do one of the following.

  • Click Marketplace in the top-level navigation.
  • Append /home to the server address in your browser’s location bar. Meaning, if the storefront address was http://marketplace.example.com, you would go instead to
    https://marketplace.example.com/home.

Step 3. ( Optional ) Do any of the following.

  • Click one of the product tiles on a carousel — such as the Featured Apps carousel or the Popular Apps carousel — to learn more about the product that it cites.

NOTE: Your preferred marketplace might have different UI carousels than the ones cited here. Also, these illustrations might not match exactly the look and feel of carousels on your preferred marketplace.

 

      • Click a category label to start filtering which products the marketplace describes to you. For example, you might click Marketing to see a listing of just the products that your marketplace administrators consider marketing-related.
      • When the categorical product listing loads — being in this example a page called Marketing — a set of tabs above the listing help you to set a basis for sorting. You can sort products by their relevance, their popularity, or their consumer rating (on a marketplace that lets its consumers submit product ratings).
      • Use pagination controls to step through listing pages.
      • Check ( tick ) any number of checkboxes in the Filters area. We apply the combination of all checked filters instantaneously to trim the set of products that a listing page describes.

      Step 4. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Sort product listings

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to https:// {marketplace} /listing , where {marketplace} is the fully qualified and DNS resolvable server name for a marketplace that you use.

      2. Step 2. Choose an option from the Sort By list.

The options are:

      • Relevance
      • Popularity
      • Newest
      • Alphabetical

Your selection takes effect immediately, re-sorting the list.

      Step 3. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Compare product listings

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to https:// {marketplace} /listing , where {marketplace} is the fully qualified and DNS resolvable server name for your marketplace instance.

      2. Step 2. Search, filter, and sort the list to limit which products it exposes. Then, check the Compare checkbox for each product that you hope to compare directly.

The state of the Compare button (above your filtered and sorted list) changes from dimmed to active after you designate multiple products to compare. The activated button is now responsive to clicking.

      Step 3. Click Compare.

      Step 4. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
