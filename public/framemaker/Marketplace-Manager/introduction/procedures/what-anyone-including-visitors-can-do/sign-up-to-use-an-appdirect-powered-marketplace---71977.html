Sign up to use an AppDirect-powered marketplace

There are two phases to user account registration.

    • Register as a new user
    • Activate your registered account
Register as a new user

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to https:// {marketplace} , where {marketplace} is the fully qualified and DNS resolvable server name for your marketplace instance.

      2. Step 2. Click Log in on the global navigation bar.

      Step 3. Click Create an Account.

The Sign Up page opens.

      Step 4. Respond to its prompts to enter your email address and — when anti-spam measures are enabled — complete a Captcha.

TIP: A Captcha is a simple test, which aims to prove that you are not the automated software that criminals sometimes use for fraud.

      Step 5. Respond to any prompts that ask you to enter unique identifiers.

TIP: This step might not apply to you.

    • Does a prompt ask you to enter your account number?

Some providers expect that you already have a billing relationship with them. For example, perhaps your marketplace provider also supplies your telephone or Internet connection. In such a case, your provider might support the convenience of charging XaaS purchases to your established account. Or, its standard policy might be to unify all of your transactions into one account.
    • Does a prompt ask you to enter a national ID for your associated company?

Some providers solicit and collect a national ID from you during registration. This ID is not tied to you as an individual person, but rather to your associated company. For example, companies that do business in Australia all have a national ID number called either an ABN or an ACN. Asking you for this ID constitutes a reasonable effort (for XaaS licensing purposes) to confirm that you are based in the correct locale.

We cross-check this value against a governmental registry to ensure that it is valid for your associated company. When it is not valid, you must correct it and try again, or your attempt to register a new user account fails.

      Step 6. Click Create an Account.

      Step 7. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

What to do next

    • Activate your registered account

Activate your registered account

Most AppDirect-powered marketplaces are configured to send an activation link to you in email when you first register, to verify that your email address is correct and valid.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Click the activation link in your email message.

      2. Step 2. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
