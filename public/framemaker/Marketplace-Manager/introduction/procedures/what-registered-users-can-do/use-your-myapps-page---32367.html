Work with your products in the Pending state

You can freely delete an order during the brief interval, if any, after you place it but before the product’s vendor — Microsoft or Google, let’s say — provisions an account for you.

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to MyApps.

The tile for a pending product shows a message and a trash can icon when your pointer hovers over it. The message says, “Our system is processing your purchase. Thanks for your patience.”

      Step 2. Click the trash can icon to cancel the pending purchase.

      Step 3. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Work with your products in the Active state

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to MyApps.

      2. Step 2. Do one or more of the following.

        • Do you want to buy an app?

      Buy an app

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

            2. b. Choose Buy.

        • Do you want to manage the settings for an entitlement?

      Manage a product

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

            2. b. Choose Manage App.

        • Do you want to view your login credentials for a product?

      View login credentials

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

            2. b. Choose View Credentials.

        • Do you want to edit your login credentials for a product?

      Edit your login credentials for a product

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

            2. b. Choose Edit Credentials.

        • Do you want to start a webapp?

      Start a webapp

          1. a. Locate and click the product tile.

            2. b. Depending on how you configured your browser, the webapp opens in a new window or on a new browser tab.
        • Do you want to edit your product login credentials?

      Update your product credentials

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

      NOTE: Some app tiles do not show any settings menu to end-users. Unless you are a company administrator, the settings menu is missing on the tile for any app that your associated company administrators manage for you on your behalf.

            b. Choose Edit Credentials.

      We prompt you to enter your credentials for the product. Most products ask only for your username and password, but some ask for additional information.

      TIP: Click the eye icon ( ) under the Password field to view the characters in your password while you enter them. Otherwise, we substitute typographical bullets for all characters in the Password field.

            c. Enter your updated credentials. Then, click Save.
        • Do you want to customize the settings for your account with the product vendor?

      Configure custom settings for your account

      Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

      Choose Configure Custom Settings.

        • Do you want to remove an app tile from your MyApps page?

      Remove a tile

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

      A settings menu (this cog icon: ) presents to you a list of all options that apply to your chosen app. Each app has its own settings menu and its own set of listed options.

      NOTE: Some app tiles do not show any settings menu to end-users. Unless you are a company administrator, the settings menu is missing on the tile for any app that your associated company administrators manage for you on your behalf.

            b. Choose Remove App.

      We prompt you to confirm your choice.

            c. Click Remove Application.
        • Do you want to run desktop software?

      Download client software to run the product locally

      NOTE: Webapps do not have downloadable versions.

      Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

      Choose Download App.

        • Do you want to upgrade?

      Upgrade to a higher edition of the same product

      Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

      Choose Upgrade Subscription.

        • Do you want to pay an overdue invoice?

      Regain product access by submitting your past-due payment

      Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

      Choose Submit Payment.

      Step 3. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Work with your products in the Expiring state

Procedure

    1. Step 1. Go to MyApps.

      2. Step 2. Do one or more of the following.

        • Do you want to start a webapp?

      Start a webapp

          1. a. Locate and click the product tile.

      TIP: You can continue to use an expiring product until its expiration takes full effect.

      Depending on how you configured your browser, the webapp opens in a new window or on a new browser tab.
        • Do you want to renew your expiring subscription?

      Renew a subscription

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

            2. b. Choose Renew Subscription.

        • Do you want to stops the expiration by submitting a payment?

      Submit a payment

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile.

            2. b. Choose Submit Payment.

      Step 3. Stop. You have completed this procedure.

Work with your products in the Blocked state

Procedure

      2. Step 2. Do one or more of the following.

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile.

      Install an extension

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

            2. b. Choose Install Extension.

        • Do you want to enter product login credentials?

      Add your product credentials

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

            2. b. Choose Add Credentials.

            c. Enter your credentials. Then, click Save.
        • Do you want to configure a product’s settings?

      Configure an app

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

            2. b. Choose Configure App.

        • Do you want to download the product’s client software for local installation?

      Download an app

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

            2. b. Choose Download App.

        • Do you want to renew the subscription?

      Renew your subscription

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

            2. b. Choose Renew Subscription.

        • Do you want to delete the product from your MyApps page?

      Remove an app

          1. a. Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

            2. b. Choose Remove App.

      Step 3. Stop. You have completed this procedure.
