Unauthenticated

Role

Description

Assignment

visitor

An unauthenticated window-shopper.

A visitor can see and browse your product selections, and might someday — through registering, activating, and authenticating his or her user account — become an end-user , a developer , a reseller , or an administrator.

The visitor role is implicit, not explicit. It is not assigned to anyone.

Consumers

Role

Description

Assignment

end-user

Has a registered account with your marketplace, and is always associated with a company that buys products from your marketplace.

A logged-in end-user can search for products, initiate free trials, request access to his or her company's purchased products, and use any product assignments received from a company administrator who oversees his or her associated company's activity on your marketplace.

An end-user does not have any procurement or administrative authority.

Every registered user account must have at least one assigned user role. The end-user role is often assigned to all registered user accounts by default because it grants the least possible access that an authenticated user can have while still consuming products.

A channel administrator can revoke this role assignment for, and restore it explicitly to, any registered user account.

billing administrator

Manages a given company's financial relationship with a marketplace. In addition to standard end-user permissions, this user has confidential access to his or her company's billing information, and can take over billing, on behalf of his or her associated company, for any product contract that any of the company’s associated end-users ordered personally.

Does not have any access to user data.

TIP: Anyone with the company administrator user role is also assigned the billing administrator user role automatically, for the same company.

These roles have conditional authority to assign the billing administrator role:

    • company administrator
    • customer support representative
    • sales support representative
    • reseller
    • channel administrator
    • corporate administrator

company administrator

Typically the person from a given company who first establishes its overall relationship with your marketplace. In addition to standard end-user permissions, he or she can invite the company's staff to join your marketplace as end-users.

Company administrators can order products, assign them to any end-users whose accounts they oversee, and set rules that govern what their associated end-users can do. Company administrators can access and change all of their company's data.

Each company administrator has limited authority to manage user permissions within his or her associated company.

These roles have conditional authority to assign the company administrator role:

    • company administrator
    • customer support representative
    • sales support representative
    • reseller
    • channel administrators
    • corporate administrators

Vendors

Role

Description

Assignment

developer

Can build and edit products, request to integrate these products into your marketplace, and accept or decline your request to offer his or her products for sale.

A user with this role has authenticated access to his or her company's own products and API integration events.

Developers populate their own product profiles, subject to oversight by a channel administrator. In some cases, a developer can also see and work with information about his or her own bills, customers, and payouts.

These roles have conditional authority to assign the developer role:

    • channel administrator
    • corporate administrator

Support staff

Role

Description

Assignment

tester

Can bypass payment when placing orders solely for testing purposes.

NOTE: A product’s vendor — Microsoft or Google, let’s say — is advised of each order that has a TESTER flag attached to it.

Corporate administrators alone have the authority to assign the tester role.

customer support representative

Can create registered companies on a marketplace and invite their first user to register and activate an associated user account.

Can administer user account settings for all registered users on a marketplace.

These roles have conditional authority to assign the customer support representative role:

    • channel administrators
    • corporate administrators

sales support representative

Can create registered companies on a marketplace and invite their first user to register and activate an associated user account.

Can buy products and bundles on any user's behalf.

These roles have conditional authority to assign the sales support representative role:

    • channel administrators
    • corporate administrators

product support representative

Has priviliges that are midway between a developer and a channel administrator , with full access to edit any product at any time. This role's chief function is to help developers during the development and integration of their products.

In addition, this role can manage the product catalog for one marketplace.

These roles have conditional authority to assign the product support representative role:

    • channel administrators
    • corporate administrators

Resellers

Role

Description

Assignment

reseller

Can create registered companies on a marketplace and invite their first user to register and activate an associated user account.

Can be linked manually to any number of registered companies by their respective company administrators , and then unlinked from any or all of them as needed.

A reseller can buy products and bundles — but only with permission — on behalf of any user whose company he or she created, or whose company administrator established a reseller link.

He or she has correspondingly limited authority to search for and view account and order details.

These roles have conditional authority to assign the reseller role:

    • reseller manager
    • channel administrator
    • corporate administrator

Marketplace administrators

Role

Description

Assignment

reseller administrator

Oversees all resellers and their links to customers and orders on the platform.

A reseller administrator must be part of a company whose “Channel Access” flag is enabled.

Only a channel administrator or a corporate administrator can assign the reseller administrator role.

channel administrator

Has near-total authority over one marketplace instance, and near-total access to its data.

This role can manage and oversee virtually every detail of its strategy and daily operation, subject to any governance by a corporate administrator.

Corporate administrators alone have the authority to assign the channel administrator role.

corporate administrator

Has total authority over multiple marketplaces, overseeing them centrally on behalf of their corporate parent.

This role can overrule a channel administrator's actions on any child marketplace that is subject to its authority, and can perform any action that a channel administrator has authority to perform.

Corporate administrators alone have the authority to assign the corporate administrator role.
